Restaurant Repo

Welcome...Why Restaurant Repo?

There are countless frameworks out there to help you build your website today. There are frameworks that are the equivalent of a Costco warehouse, with everything you could possibly need and then some. There are also frameworks that are like going to your mom and pop grocery store on the corner, giving you just the basics, that you need to make a quick dinner. Restaurant Repo is a framework that fits just in the middle of those two, we can get your website up and running quickly, but with more than just the bare bones. We offer a variety of options for styling your website coupled with some efficient interactivity so you can maximize your customer's experience and deliver your bottomline. We are offering you great solutions for showcasing the best of your business on the world wide web, all packaged nicely in an easy to use format. We are focused on your web needs within the hospitality industry.

Where To Start

The Basics

  • HTML

    We set up our pages using HTML5, using the most current doctype, and set up to be built responsively. Download our template

  • Interactivity

    The interactivity of our components are built with javascript and jQuery. jQuery can be applied with the latest CDN. Download our template

  • Fonts and Icons

    We have a selection of fonts and icons that we recommend for our pages, but you can choose to use as many or as little as your prefer.

    We like to use GoogleFonts because they have a large selection, you wil be able to find almost any style to suit your needs. We like to use FontAwesome for our icons. Like GoogleFonts, they have a huge selection and are easy to use. Both GoogleFonts and FontAwesome are available through CDN. Download our template
